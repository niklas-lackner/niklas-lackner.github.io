I am a medical physicist working in the Department of Radiology at the University Hospital Erlangen, Friedrich-Alexander-Universität Erlangen–Nürnberg.

My research focuses on advancing computed tomography for radiotherapy and diagnostic imaging, with a primary emphasis on photon-counting CT, particularly spectral imaging applications, and on the advancement of imaging dose methods in CT. Complementary research areas include respiratory motion handling, automated quality assurance, and the integration of artificial intelligence into computed tomography and hospital imaging workflows. A substantial part of my work is conducted within the framework of <a href="https://racoon.network/">RACOON</a>, where I contribute to multicenter imaging studies, standardized analysis pipelines, and translational imaging research.
