## RACOON team Erlangen

RACOON is the nationwide network of all university radiology departments and partner institutes in Germany, dedicated to the structured collection, harmonization, and analysis of radiological data for multicenter research and innovation.

Within the RACOON consortium, coordination tasks are distributed across several sites: Erlangen serves as the hub for scientific coordination, Berlin is responsible for technical coordination, Tübingen leads public relations activities, and Frankfurt oversees regulatory affairs.

At the University Hospital Erlangen, the local RACOON team contributes to both strategic and operational aspects of the network. Prof. Dr. Michael Uder represents Erlangen in the RACOON Steering Committee. Prof. Dr. Matthias May acts as Coordination Lead and Site Manager. Project management is supported by Lea Seufferth, while IT infrastructure and technical implementation are handled by Sebastian Arndt, with additional project management and IT support provided by Mats Bente. I contribute expertise in CT physics and support multiple RACOON subprojects, with a particular focus on imaging methodology and multicenter study implementation. In addition, several colleagues—including Jonas, Ruben, Simon, Theresa, and others—contribute to scientific projects within RACOON across different domains.
